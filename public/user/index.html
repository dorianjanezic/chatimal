Welcome to Animal Talk. This is a messaging application that translates your messages to animal language. To start, select the animal that will communicate your message to another user. Write your name, your message, and press send. When you receive a message from another user, press "Hear Messages" to listen to the animal they chose to communicate through.

This project sources sounds from Macaulay Library's archive of recordings and Brian House's Urban Intonation.
